This package contains 23 props in total with detailed squared textures of
1024 and 2048 pixels.
18 props have different Levels of Detail (Unity 3.5 Pro needed) making 
41 meshes in total.


How to use:
If you are a Unity 3.5 Pro user, use the "LOD prefabs" folder with the LODed meshes.
If you don't want to use LODed prefabs or you don't have Unity Pro, use the meshes from
"meshes" folder, choosing the LOD that fits better your needs.
